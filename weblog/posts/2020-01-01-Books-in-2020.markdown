## Technical

### Professional CMake: A Practical Guide

*Craig SCOTT*

A reference manual for CMake. I was hoping for something more like a project-based walkthrough, but it is very thorough and a useful reference on many topics. Used in conjunction with Google, I'm able to do what I want in ways that feel simple and maintainable enough (using CMake to build a C++ monorepo).

*Read in Progress*

## Career

### Developer Hegemony

> The Future of Labor

*Erik Dietrich*

The author's thoughts on how to manage one's career as a programmer, and his observations and experiences from working in large companies and startups.
